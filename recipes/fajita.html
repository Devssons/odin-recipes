Fajitas

Fast and easy-made fajitas with chicken

Ingredients

  • 500 gram chicken
  • 2 Onions
  • Cucumber
  • Corn
  • Sallad
  • Red bellpepper
  • Pack of Fajita spices

Steps

  1. Cut all the vegetables in thin slices
  2. Fry the chicken
  3. Add the bellpepper and onions to the frying pan
  4. Add the fajita spices
